# **Platform Guidelines: Cold Brew Coffee Co.**

**Purpose:** This document ensures all content is correctly formatted and styled per platform before publishing. Reference this before finalizing any post, video, or caption. Covers specs, tone adjustments, and formatting rules for each active platform.

---

## **1\. Platform Overview**

| Platform | Content Type | Primary Tone | Hashtags |
| ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Instagram** | Image posts, Stories | Aspirational, polished, punchy | Required (3–5) |
| **Threads** | Short text posts | Witty, conversational, playful | Optional |
| **YouTube** | Long & short-form video | Informative, energetic, premium | Via tags/description |

---

## **2\. Instagram**

### **Specs**

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Feed Post** | 1080 × 1080 px | 1:1 |
| **Story** | 1080 × 1920 px | 9:16 |
| **Reel Cover** | 1080 × 1920 px | 9:16 |

### **Caption Guidelines**

* **Length:** Short — 1 to 3 sentences max before the hashtag block  
* **Structure:** Hook → Value/Vibe → CTA → Line break → Hashtags  
* **Emojis:** 1–2 from approved set (see `brand_identity.md`)  
* **CTA:** Always include one; place before hashtags

**Example caption structure:**

Your morning called. It wants an upgrade. ☕

Shop Now →

\#ColdBrewCoffeeCo \#ColdBrew \#MorningFuel \#BrewedDifferent \#CoffeeCulture

### **Hashtag Rules**

* Use **3–5 hashtags** per post  
* Place hashtags **after the CTA**, separated by a line break  
* Mix brand (`#ColdBrewCoffeeCo`) \+ product (`#ColdBrew`) \+ lifestyle (`#MorningFuel`) tags  
* Refer to `brand_identity.md` → Section 5 for approved hashtag list

### **Content Notes**

* Always use high-resolution assets from the approved visual library  
* For Stories: keep text in the **center safe zone** (avoid top/bottom 250px)  
* Product must be visible in the first frame

## **3\. Threads**

### **Specs**

* **Text only** (images optional)  
* **Max length:** 500 characters  
* No mandatory dimensions — if attaching image, use 1:1 or 4:5

### **Caption Guidelines**

* **Tone:** Witty, casual, conversational — this is the most human-sounding platform  
* **Length:** 1–3 short sentences; punchy and self-contained  
* **Hashtags:** Optional — use 1 at most if included; never lead with hashtags  
* **No hard CTA required** — can end with a question, observation, or punchline

**Example post styles:**

Hot coffee in summer is a personality trait. Not ours, but someone's. ☕

Cold brew doesn't need to be heated up. Neither do you. 💥

Unpopular opinion: waiting for coffee to brew is just unnecessary suspense.

### **Content Notes**

* Avoid sounding like an ad — Threads rewards authenticity and wit  
* Engagement bait ("comment below\!") is discouraged; let the copy do the work  
* Can repurpose Instagram captions but must be shortened and made more casual

## **4\. YouTube**

### **Specs**

| Format | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio | Min Resolution |
| ----- | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Standard Video** | 1920 × 1080 px | 16:9 | 1080p |
| **Shorts** | 1080 × 1920 px | 9:16 | 1080p |
| **Thumbnail** | 1280 × 720 px | 16:9 | 72 dpi |

### **Title Guidelines**

* **Length:** 60–70 characters (avoids truncation in search)  
* **Structure:** Descriptive phrase \+ SEO keyword(s)  
* **Style:** Clear and informative first, clever second  
* **No clickbait** — titles should accurately reflect content

**Example titles:**

Cold Brew at Home: The Easiest Morning Upgrade | Cold Brew Coffee Co.  
How to Start Your Morning Right with Cold Brew Coffee  
The Smoothest Cold Brew You'll Ever Try — No Bitterness Guaranteed

### **Description Guidelines**

* **Length:** 2–3 sentences  
* **Structure:** What the video is about → Key product benefit → CTA with link  
* Include relevant keywords naturally (cold brew, ready-to-drink coffee, morning routine)  
* Always end with a CTA

**Example description:**

Upgrade your morning routine with Cold Brew Coffee Co. — smooth, low-acid,   
ready-to-drink cold brew that fits any schedule. No bitterness, no brewing,   
just great coffee. Shop now at \[link\].

### **Thumbnail Guidelines**

* Product or person must be clearly visible at small sizes  
* Use bold, legible text overlay (max 6 words)  
* High contrast — avoid busy or dark backgrounds  
* Match thumbnail energy to video tone

### **Content Notes**

* Videos should open with a hook in the **first 3 seconds**  
* Shorts follow the same rules as Reels — fast pacing, product visible early  
* Always include a verbal or on-screen CTA before the video ends
