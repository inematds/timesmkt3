Marketing Angles

Angle 01 PRIMARY

Easter Morning, Upgraded

"Most people wake up to chaos on Easter. You wake up to cold brew."

Position cold brew as the premium Easter morning ritual. Connects to universal holiday morning chaos and the desire for smooth, effortless energy. Mirrors our core product truth.

Angle 02

The Easter Gift Nobody Returns

"Chocolate? Flowers? Try again. Cold brew actually wins."

Leverages millennial Easter gifting behavior (26% buy gifts). Humor differentiates from generic holiday copy. High shareability on Threads and Instagram.

Smooth as Easter Sunday

"Easter Sunday. No chaos. No bitterness. Just smooth."

Easter's cultural association with calm and renewal mirrors cold brew's smooth, low-acid profile. Strong awareness / brand-building angle.

FOMO + scarcity framing. Even without a product variant, the campaign feels exclusive. Gen Z responds to seasonal limited-edition narratives.
